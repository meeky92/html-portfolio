A Leap

Why Coding?

I've been an ICU nurse for a little over 7 years and have wanted to leave bedside nursing for some time. Several long years of feeling burned out that were exacerbated by the pandemic pushed things over the edge for me. I imagine many of us have gone through a similar feeling. I initially looked into other non bedside nursing roles and was extremely discouraged during that process as I couldn't get jobs that I was excited about. I wasn't sure that I was ready for a career change as I've only been in healthcare and wasn't confident I had transferrable skills.

I'm fortunate to have friends in other interesting fields and was intrigued by software engineering. Frankly, I'm not entirely sure I'm suited for this world, but I am drawn to the field's stability and its never ending use of problem solving. I didn't think that a coding bootcamp would be something I could or should attempt but I figured trying something terrifying and failing was still a better idea than returning to an ICU. I'm lucky to have support from family and I'm excited to see how I'll grow these next couple of months.
